Services

Insured and licensed New Jersey

CS&E Systems provides custom services for both commercial and residential installation projects.

We will travel to most NJ locations to give a no-obligation estimate for project-type work.

We offer a wide variety of custom installation options with over 20-years of proud service.

Entertainment

Wide range of products and services makes putting together the best set-up difficult. There are thousands of great products that can make up the optimal system that is not too expensive or too low in quality. We make suggestions, get you easy to understand answers. The goal is to help buyers make good decisions and then create that system for them and their family.

Security

We provide state if the art cameras and recording devices. Many cameras record now to the cloud making it easier to view information at a fingers touch.

WIFI & Cellular

We design WIFI systems that work with your providers gateway. There are many ways to get good internet. Quality routers, access points and poe switches are available at most price points and are worth every cent.
